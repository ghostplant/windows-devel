常用控件

以TreeView为例：

CTreeCtrl *m_Tree;

*m_Tree=(CTreeCtrl *)GetDlgItem(IDC_TREE_NAME);

m_Tree->InsertItem(...);

其他控件的类：

CListCtrl; CRichEditView; CImageList; CButton; CHtmlView; CListBox; CEdit; CProgressCtrl; CSliderCtrl; CTabCtrl; CStatic; CAnimateCtrl。

常用控件的风格常数

Windows: WS_[MAX/MIN]IMIZEBOX|WS_CHILD|WS_PUPOL|WS_DLGFRAME|WS_THICKFRAME|WS_VISIBLE|WS_TABSTOP|WS_BORDER|WS_OVERLAPPEDWINDOW|WS_VSCROLL|WS_HSCROLL|WS_CAPTION

Buttons: BS_(DEF)PUSTBUTTON|BS_(AUTO)RADIOBUTTON|BS_(AUTO)CHECKBOX

Statics: SS_LEFT(NOWORDWRAP)|SS_WHITEFRAME|SS_WHITERECT|

EditBox: ES_MULTILINE|ES_WANTRETURN|ES_AUTOVSCROLL

ComboBox: CBS_AUTOHSCROLL|CBS_SORT|CBS_DROP(DOWN)|CBS_SIMPLE

ListCtrl: LVS_NOSCROLL|LVS_ICON|LVS_SMALLICON|LVS_LIST|LVS_REPORT

TreeCtrl: TVS_HASLINES|TVS_HASBUTTONS|TVS_EDITLABLES|TVS_SHOWSELALWAYS
